What is MUN?

Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations where students play the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real world issues with the policies and perspectives of their assigned country.

This Year's Theme: Unravelling the Domino Effect

'Unraveling the Domino Effect,' beckons us to explore the intricate interplay of global decisions and their profound consequences. Much like a single falling domino initiates a captivating chain reaction, our theme encourages us to delve into the far-reaching impacts of diplomatic actions.
